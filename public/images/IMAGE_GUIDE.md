# Image Guide for Wedding Website

This guide explains which images you need and where to place them.

├── timeline/

│   ├── 2-first-date.jpg           (First date photo)

## Image Specifications

### Hero Images
- **wedding-hero.jpg**: 1920x1080px or higher (landscape)
  - This is the main background on the home page
  - Should be a romantic/wedding-themed image

- **love-story-bg.jpg**: 1920x1080px or higher (landscape)
  - Background for "Our Love Story" section
  - Should work well with dark overlay

### Couple Photos
- **bride.jpg & groom.jpg**: 600x600px or higher (square preferred)
  - These will be displayed in circular frames
  - Clear headshots or upper body photos work best
  - Make sure the face is centered

### Timeline Images (Our Story Page)
- All: 800x600px or higher (landscape 4:3 ratio)
- These tell your story chronologically:
  1. First meet - Photo from when you first met
  2. First date - Photo from your first date
  3. Adventures - Travel/adventure photos together
  4. Growing together - Photos of milestone moments
  5. Families - Photos with both families
  6. Proposal - The proposal moment or engagement photos

### Gallery Images
- All: 600x600px or higher (square format works best)
- Mix of candid and posed photos
- These will be displayed in a grid on the Our Story page
- Can be any memorable moments from your relationship

## Image Format
- Recommended: JPG or JPEG
- PNG also supported but larger file sizes
- Keep individual file sizes under 2MB for faster loading

## How to Add Your Images

1. Save your photos with the exact filenames listed above
2. Place them in the corresponding folders
3. The website will automatically use your images!

## Need Help?
- Use free tools like Canva or Photoshop to resize images
- For circular crops (couple photos), make sure faces are centered
- Compress large images using tools like TinyPNG.com
